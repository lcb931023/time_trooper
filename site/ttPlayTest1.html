Play Test 1

Play test 1 was geared more towards the games mechanics in a fast paced development environment with very little focus on any of the graphical assets for the Time Troopers. We really wanted to get the time manipulation mechanic in so we can really see how our classmates will respond.

Goals

  • Character Movement
  • Time Manipulation
  • Bullet Generation
  • Hit Detection
  • Character Sprite

  • View our Goals on Github

Postive Results

"The art is very nice and the time changing mechanics are cool."
"Cool looking game! Put in the ability to walk back and forth."
"Graphics are nice looking. I like how its in the browser, not some .exe file"

Negative Results

"The bullets at the top of the screen are pointless since you can't jump that high."
"Some of the bullets are impossible to avoid because the character can't crouch."
"You can spam jumping and time changing."
"Bullets near the top are useless as is, also puts you into some situations where you're forced to take a hit"
"I'd much rather see total points than total hits."
"Sometimes bullets spawn in a way that makes it impossible to avoid, even with the change in time."

      • WHAT'S BEHIND OUR NAME?

      What happens when you put 4 skilled seniors who are tired of the grind of college classes in a group? A great team who to make classes more enjoyable apply a comedic and relaxed attitude towards everything! Our name derived from a statement the lovely Professor Elouise Oyzon said to us: "You guys do such great work, you just need to take it a little more serious."

      Seriously Joking: We are serious about our work, just not our attitudes.
