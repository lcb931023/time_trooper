Play Test 2

Play test 2 is where we really got into the nitty gritty. Our first play test was really just an experiment to see if our game would be any good. A lot of the "negative" comments were things we didn't address or even bother doing, but knew we had to get added before this next play test. There were no "negative" comments about our main mechanic of Time Manipulation. So for this play test we really wanted to give our classmates a game to play, not just test for us again like our minions.

Goals

  • Character sprite sheet
  • Graphic improvements
  • Change controls to arrow keys
  • Distance Tracking (Score)
  • High Score!
  • Limit duration of time manipulation

  • View our Goals on Github

Postive Results

"This game feels really good."
"I really like the way it looks."
"The high score makes the game addicting."

Negative Results

"I don't know how to tell if I'm going to die, make health apparent."
"The response time for crouching lags after a jump."
"What's the point of speeding up time? Why would I want to do this instead of slowing it down."
"Control scheme is a little awkward."
"You can crouch under almost everything."
"Back leg sticking out when jumping."
