Time Trooper

Time Trooper is a 2D canvas game created by Elbin Shin, Changbai Li, JJ Watson, and Mike Nguyen for GDDII. The game features one main character with the ability to manipulate time to get past the obstacles in her way. The objective is to get as far as you can without dying and to set new records!

Controls

Jump
Crouch/Slide
Speed up time
Slow down time

      • WHAT'S BEHIND OUR NAME?

      What happens when you put 4 skilled seniors who are tired of the grind of college classes in a group? A great team who to make classes more enjoyable apply a comedic and relaxed attitude towards everything! Our name derived from a statement the lovely Professor Elouise Oyzon said to us: "You guys do such great work, you just need to take it a little more serious."
